Welcome to PVR High School

PVR High School, established in 2012, is committed to providing quality education while nurturing discipline, values, and holistic growth.

Our Mission

To nurture young minds with knowledge, skills, and values, enabling them to face future challenges with confidence and integrity.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in education by inspiring passion, shaping vision, and helping students achieve remarkable results.

Messages

Chairperson's Message

Education is the foundation of society, and at PVR High School, we are dedicated to shaping students who are not only academically strong but also socially responsible and morally upright. We strive to create an environment that fosters curiosity, creativity, and a lifelong love for learning.

Principal's Message

At PVR, we emphasize academic excellence, character development, and overall personality growth. Our teachers are mentors who encourage students to explore their interests, participate in extracurricular activities, and become confident individuals ready to face global challenges.
