History

PVR High School was established in 2012 with the aim of providing quality education that nurtures intellect, character, and creativity. Our school focuses on developing students academically, socially, and emotionally, preparing them to excel in life and become responsible citizens.

Mission

To provide a holistic education that empowers students to achieve their fullest potential through academic excellence, moral guidance, and extra-curricular engagement.

Vision

To be a leading institution recognized for excellence in education, fostering innovation, ethical values, and lifelong learning in every student.

Chairperson's Message

Welcome to PVR High School! Our commitment is to guide students toward success by combining quality education with moral and social development. Every child is encouraged to explore, learn, and excel.

Principal's Message

Our vision is to create an inspiring and supportive environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. We take pride in nurturing young minds to achieve excellence.
