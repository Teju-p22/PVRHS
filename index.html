History

Founded in 2012, PVR High School was established with the goal of providing high-quality education in a nurturing environment. We emphasize holistic development, blending academics with extracurricular activities, and fostering values that prepare students for future success.

Mission

To cultivate an inspiring environment where every student can achieve academic excellence, develop critical thinking, and become responsible citizens.

Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for innovation, integrity, and fostering lifelong learning in every student.

Chairperson's Message

Welcome to PVR High School! Our focus is to create a nurturing space for students to grow academically and personally. We believe in inspiring curiosity, resilience, and a love for learning.

Principal's Message

At PVR High School, we are committed to delivering quality education and fostering the unique talents of every student. Our faculty ensures guidance, discipline, and a safe learning environment.
