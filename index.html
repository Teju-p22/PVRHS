History

PVR High School was established in 2012 with a strong commitment to provide quality education to children in Vanaparthy and surrounding areas. Over the years, the school has grown into a hub for holistic development, nurturing students academically, socially, and emotionally. Our vision has always been to cultivate curiosity, responsibility, and integrity among our students, preparing them to thrive in an ever-changing world.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver a comprehensive educational experience, combining strong academics with co-curricular activities, instilling moral values, and fostering critical thinking. We aim to empower students to become lifelong learners and responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

Vision

We envision a school environment where every student is encouraged to explore, innovate, and achieve excellence. By providing support, guidance, and opportunities, PVR High School aspires to nurture confident individuals ready to face global challenges.
