History

PVR High School was established in 2012 with a vision to provide holistic and quality education to students of all backgrounds. Since its inception, the school has focused on fostering intellectual curiosity, moral values, and creative thinking. Our nurturing environment encourages students to excel academically, socially, and emotionally. With dedicated faculty and committed leadership, we aim to empower students to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

Mission

To provide a comprehensive learning experience that encourages intellectual, social, and emotional development in students, preparing them for success in all walks of life.

Vision

To cultivate a generation of confident, responsible, and compassionate individuals who contribute positively to society and lead with integrity.

Admissions

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Nursery: 3-4 years
  • LKG: 4-5 years
  • UKG: 5-6 years
  • Grades 1-10: as per government norms

Admission Process:

  1. Submit the official school admission form with required documents (birth certificate, previous school transfer certificate, photos).
  2. Documents are verified by school staff.
  3. Interaction/assessment for higher grades (if applicable).
  4. Confirmation of admission after verification and payment of fees.
  5. Orientation session for new students and parents.
